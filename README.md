# Global-Temperature-Change-Analysis
1. Project Name
   
Analysis of Global Temperature Change Using Data-Driven Methods

2. Overview

Climate change has become one of the most pressing global challenges of the 21st century. Rising
global temperatures have significant consequences for ecosystems, agriculture, water resources, and
human health. The increase in greenhouse gas emissions, rapid industrialization, and unsustainable
resource consumption have accelerated global warming, leading to more frequent extreme weather
events such as heat waves, droughts, and floods.
Understanding historical temperature change patterns is an essential step in addressing the climate
crisis. By analyzing long-term temperature trends across countries and regions, researchers and
policymakers can better understand how climate conditions have evolved over time. Such insights are
important for designing effective climate policies and developing predictive models that can forecast
future climate scenarios.
The main objective of this project is to analyze global temperature change data using data-driven
methods. Through the analysis of historical temperature records, the project aims to uncover
patterns, trends, and variations across countries, years, and months. The results of this analysis can
contribute to a better understanding of climate change dynamics and provide a foundation for future
artificial intelligence or machine learning models that aim to predict climate trends.
